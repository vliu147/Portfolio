What is this Project?

The DailyFocus web-app is a project that lets users create their own accounts, which links to their emails and can be sent reminders about goals they have created. The app allows for dynamic customizaion through adjusable tables, designated filters and setting changes, such as deadline notification frequency and filtering goals by subjects. There is also a stastics page to get an overview of every goal previously set.

Why did we choose this?

This project was created to encourage people with busy schedules to keep track of goals they make and be motivated to complete them. This app would give you reminders and have you set reasons for your goals so that the user can always be reminded why they started or created their goal in the first place. originally, this was intended for college students as they are in a big transitional part of their lives and often have a lot going on, but can be used by anyone.

How does it work?

The app works by first having users create an account that is linked with firebase on the backend. This creates a unique id for the individual and can be tracked for any potential administrative changes needed for the user. The email used in account creation is then conncted with SendGrid to allow for emails to be sent whenever the user chooses. Within the app contains main and subtasks that can be be organized by creation date, priority or other filter settings.
